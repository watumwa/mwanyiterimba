Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Home / Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

Mwanyi Terimba Limited ("we", "us", "our") is a private company registered in Uganda. Our registered office is at Muganzirwazza Commercial Complex, Katwe, Kampala. We operate the website at mwanyiterimba.com and sell Kaawa Mpologoma coffee products.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: sales@mwanyiterimba.com or call +256-778-253810.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our website or place an order, we may collect the following information:

  • Name — provided when placing an order or sending a message.
  • Phone number — provided for order confirmation and delivery coordination.
  • Delivery address — provided to arrange delivery or pickup.
  • Email address — provided optionally when contacting us.
  • Order details — products selected, pack sizes, and special instructions.

We do not collect payment card details. Payments are arranged directly through WhatsApp or in person.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process and confirm your coffee orders.
  • Arrange delivery or pickup of your order.
  • Respond to your inquiries and messages.
  • Contact you about your order status.

We do not use your information for marketing without your consent, and we do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

4. WhatsApp and Third-Party Services

Our order and contact forms send information to WhatsApp (operated by Meta Platforms, Inc.) to facilitate communication with our sales team. When you submit a form, your browser opens WhatsApp with your order or message pre-filled. WhatsApp's own privacy policy applies to that communication.

We use Google Fonts and Google Maps on this website. These services may collect usage data as described in Google's privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

Order and contact information shared via WhatsApp is retained only as long as necessary to fulfil your order or respond to your inquiry. We do not maintain a separate database of customer personal data from this website.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sales@mwanyiterimba.com.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking scripts beyond Google Fonts and Google Maps embeds.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We encourage you to contact us directly by phone if you have concerns about sharing information online.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact
